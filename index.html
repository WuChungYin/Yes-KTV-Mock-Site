About YES KTV

YES KTV is a full service and upscale karaoke bar & club in Houston, TX, USA. Our private party rooms are cozy, modern and luxuriously designed. Also, our song selections and audio/video equipment are the best in town. So, bring your party to YES KTV and enjoy.

You can bring in your own bottle if you are over 21 years old. No outside food - we sell soft drinks and Chinese food, so you can bring in alcohol only.

Large VIP Room Reservation Rules

1. To book a Large or Extra Large VIP room, a first hour deposit is required.

2. Deposit has to be made at least 7 days prior to the reservation date. (Make deposit ASAP to secure your reservation).

3. No refund will be given for cancellation within 5 calendar days prior to the scheduled reservation.
