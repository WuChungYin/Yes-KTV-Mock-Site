About YES KTV

YES KTV is a full service and upscale karaoke bar & club in Houston, TX, USA. Our private party rooms are cozy, modern and luxuriously designed. Also, our song selections and audio/video equipment are the best in town. So, bring your party to YES KTV and enjoy.

We offer:

The best private VIP rooms

The best audio and video song equipment

Low prices and the best service!
